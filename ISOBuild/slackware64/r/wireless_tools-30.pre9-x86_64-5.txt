wireless_tools: This package contains tools and a library used for configuring
wireless_tools: wireless networking interfaces:
wireless_tools:  iwconfig (the main wireless tool),  iwlist (display more detailed
wireless_tools:  information than iwconfig),  iwspy (get stats per MAC address and
wireless_tools:  more),  iwpriv (set driver private ioctls),  iwgetid (show the
wireless_tools:  ESSID or NWID of a device),  iwevent (display wireless events),
wireless_tools:  ifrename (rename a network interface).
